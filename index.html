about TrainSmart

Children with Autism and can take 18 months or more to toilet train and 35% are not trained by age 6. TrainSmart is a better approach to toilet training. Using behavior analysis techniques TrainSmart gets children potty trained quicker than tradition techniques, without freaking them out. With TrainSmart you can know when to change diapers without checking for wet diapers. Alerts trigger our intervention methodology on your phone.

About VinciTek

We are an innovative biomedical company that focuses on commercializing new toilet-training technologies and methodologies for children with developmental disabilities.  Our product has been successfully tested and has received significant Public and Private sector support. Our technology was developed and tested at the University of Rochester through grants funded by Autism Speaks and the NIH.
